/ Sample Assembly Program 4: AND Operation
/ Demonstrates logical AND and masking

        ORG 50
START,  LDA VAL1        / Load value 1
        AND MASK        / AND with mask
        STA RESULT      / Store result
        HLT
VAL1,   HEX ABCD        / Value: 0xABCD
MASK,   HEX 0FFF        / Mask: keep lower 12 bits
RESULT, HEX 0
        END
